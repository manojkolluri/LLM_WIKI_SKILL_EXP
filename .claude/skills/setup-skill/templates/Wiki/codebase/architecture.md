# Architecture

## High-level shape

_To be filled in once the product's architecture starts taking shape._

This file documents the **why** behind important structural decisions — not just what the codebase looks like, but the reasoning that led to it. Examples of what belongs here:

- Choice of framework / runtime / language and why
- Where code lives (folder layout) and why
- Auth model (client-side / middleware / SSR) and why
- Styling approach and why
- How env vars and secrets are handled
- Notable cross-cutting patterns

## Decisions and the reasoning behind them

_Add a subsection per decision as they're made. Each subsection should explain the choice AND the alternatives considered AND why this one won._

### Template for new decisions

```
### <Decision title>

<One paragraph stating the decision plainly.>

**Why:** <reasoning>

**Alternatives considered:** <other options and why they lost>

**Implications:** <what this means for future work>
```
